How it works

Your daily dose of health in 3 simple steps

01

How many oranges would you like each day?

Never again waste time thinking how to get your daily dose of vitamins. Orange AI will create a 100% personalized weekly meal plan just for you. It makes sure you get all the nutrients and vitamins you need!

02

Keeping up your vitamins

Once per week, approve the meal plan generated for you by Orange AI. You can change quantity, and if selected by type, colors and taste. We also deliver 1 free orange juice for every meal plan.

03

Receive order at convenient time

You will be provided with your selected quantity of oranges every day, and we will deliver it to your door whenever works best for you. You can change delivery schedule and address daily!
