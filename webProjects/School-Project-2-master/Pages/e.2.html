Commonwealth Games open with celebration of host city Birmingham

A 10-metre-tall raging bull made of steel marked the start of the Commonwealth Games ceremony on Thursday night with an unashamedly loud celebration in Birmingham, UK.

The opening ceremony was 18 months in the making, the work of a creative team featuring the Peaky Blinders creator Steven Knight and the Super Bowl half-time director Hamish Hamilton.

Dame Louise Martin, the president of the Commonwealth Games Federation, said she believed this year’s event would be “one of the greatest and most important editions of the Commonwealth Games in our 92-year history”.
