Purpose

The purpose of this website to allow users to read articles and get the latest news and updates, about one of the biggest event held in the United Kingdom, The Commonwealth Games. Aditionally, they can not only review all the sports that take part in the event, but also purchase tickets directly on the website in just a few clicks. The purpose of The Commonwealth Games is to build peaceful, sustainable and prosperous communities globally by inspiring Commonwealth Athletes to drive the impact and ambition of all citizens Sport.

Accesibility

I addressed accessibility by creating an adapted copy of the main page with a bigger, dyslexia friendly font and higher contrasts, saturation, and spacing. The photos grow larger as you hover them. This accessibility mode can be accessed from every page using either the "Accessibility" button in the navigation bar or the one in the footer menu. Details

On the ticket purchasing process, you can either sign up or create a new account, if you don't own one.

After writing your details, both Log in and Sign Up buttons will lead to the payment process page.


Improvements

I wish I had more time to adress the ticket purchasing process on the Form Page, as I wanted to insert a more complex way to select the days. For example, for selecting a period, the user would click on the first day of the Commonwealth Games Event on the calendar and then on the last one.
