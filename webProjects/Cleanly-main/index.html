Routine house cleaning

Our service is available either weekly or fortnightly only and minimum contractual hours are 2.5hrs weekly or 4hrs fortnightly, however discretion is used depending on the size of your home and needs. We also offer one off cleaning such as end of tenancy cleans or spring cleans.

Regular Business Cleaning

We offer business cleaning services for offices, schools, short terms rentals & restaurants. All the household cleaning features are included.

Special Cleaning Service

One-off cleaning services such as deep cleaning provided on demand; costs agreed in advance. We also offer particular cleaning services such as carpet cleaning, window cleaning, screen cleaning, communal cleaning, upholstery cleaning, gutter cleaning, matress cleaning, and laundry cleaning.
